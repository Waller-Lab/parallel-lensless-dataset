Datasets

This page provides an overview of how to use our datasets. We provide the following:

  • The original, full resolution RML, Diffusercam, and Ground Truth measurements.
  • Experimentally captured point-spread function measurements.
  • Full resolution, undistorted Ground Truth measurements.
  • Undistorted Ground Truth measurements warped to RML, at 8x downsampling.
  • Undistorted Ground Truth measurements warped to Diffusercam, at 8x downsampling.
  • Homographies mapping from Ground Truth to RML and Ground Truth to Diffusercam at 8x downsampling.
  • Example iPython Notebook for processing the original measurements for homography warping and for processing the measurements for machine learning training.
